About Me

Hi! I’m Maryam.

I’m a PhD student researching privacy and security in autonomous systems. Currently, I’m focusing on differential privacy. My work explores how to protect sensitive information in cyber-physical systems while maintaining strong privacy guarantees.

I’m passionate about building privacy-preserving technologies that don’t compromise system performance.
